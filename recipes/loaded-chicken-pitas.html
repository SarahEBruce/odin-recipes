Loaded Chicken Pitas

Description

If you are looking for an easy nutritious lunch idea that packs a flavour punch, look no further than these stuffed pita pockets.

With minimal preparation, these can be made ahead of time and are ready to give you 25 grams of protein for under 360 calories in neat and portable, and most importantly delicious package.

Ingredients

  • 1 wholemeal pita bread
  • 100g chicken tikka, cooked
  • 100g fat free Greek yogurt
  • Large handful baby spinach

Steps

  1. Mix the chicken tikka and yogurt in a bowl.
  2. Toast the pita and open the pockets.
  3. Spoon in the chicken mix and pack in the spinach.
  4. Enjoy!
