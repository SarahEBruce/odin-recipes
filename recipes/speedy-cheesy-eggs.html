Speedy Cheesy Eggs

Description

This protein packed eggy breakfast is all you need in the morning to fill you up and keep you satisified until lunch!

Quick and easy and packing 34g of protein for under 450 calories, this recipe is one for hitting your goals when you're short on time.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 30g half fat graed cheddar
  • 1/4 cup baby spinach
  • 2 tbsp semi-skimmed milk
  • 5 sprays 1-cal spray
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Spray a large microwaveable mug with 1 cal spray, about 5 sprays.
  2. Break eggs into the mug and add milk, salt and pepper.
  3. Whisk until combined.
  4. Stir in spinach.
  5. Microwave for 30 seconds on high power, stir, then repeat for 30 seconds more.
  6. Top with cheese and microwave for another 15-45 seconds until eggs are cooked and cheese is melted.
  7. Tip onto toasted seeded bread and enjoy!
