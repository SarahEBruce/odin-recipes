Sweet Potato and Chickpea Curry

Description

A curry for warming the soul, not too spicy but filling and comforting for those cosy winter nights.

At less than 350 calories, this curry is a light but satisfying vegan dish that is packed with flavour and serves up a 15g portion of protein.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 2 onions, diced
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 can light coconut milk
  • 1 can chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 cup dried red lentils, rinsed
  • 1 large bag baby spinach
  • 1 jar curry paste (any flavour)
  • 10-15 sprays 1-cal spray

Steps

  1. Spray a large pan with 1-cal spray, and fry onion for 5 minutes until soft.
  2. Add sweet potato, curry paste and ginger and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add all remaining ingredients, cover and simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Divide into 8 portions, saving leftovers in the fridge or freezing to keep for longer.
  5. Enjoy!
